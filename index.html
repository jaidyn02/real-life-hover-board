Real life hoverboard

The real life hoverboard or the Hendo hoverboard was created by a California based tech form Hendo.The hover board itself can only float a inch above the ground and has a 15 minute battery life. The founders of the company Jill and Greg Henderson raised 450,000$ on Kickstarter and plan on going further. There are only 10 being made to order. There are different models made each keep improving.
